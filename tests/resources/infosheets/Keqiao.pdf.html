Taxi will be available from those airports to Keqiao. And it’s also recommended 
to take high speed train from Shanghai Hongqiao Railway Station to Shaoxing

tickets. 
Organizer will pick up at Hangzhou Xiaoshan Airport and Shaoxing North Railway 
Station to official hotel. Form Hangzhou airport takes 10＄dollars/person/one-
way，from Shaoxing North Railway Station takes 5＄dollars/person/one-way. 
For athletes and team officials who will compete in the IFSC World cup Wujiang 
(lead+speed), you could take official bus from Keqiao to Wujiang, it’s about 2 
hours by bus and takes 20＄dollars/person/one-way.
