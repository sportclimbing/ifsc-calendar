Scheduling Visa Appointments: 
• If an applicant is not able to schedule a visa appointment before their date of travel, 
they can 
request an expedited appointment. To request an expedite, the applicant must first 
schedule a 
regular appointment (even if it is beyond the date of travel), and then re-enter the 
scheduling 
system and select the "Request Expedite/Emergency Appointment" option. 
• When requesting an expedite, applicants must clearly explain their reason for travel 
and dates of 
travel to the U.S. 
• Applicants can also consider applying in a third-country location with shorter wait-
times. There is 
no penalty for applying anywhere appointments are available, even outside one's home 
country. 
 
Requirements to Travel to the United States for Foreign Travelers 
*As of May 12, 2023, noncitizen non-immigrant air passengers will no longer need to 
show proof of being fully vaccinated with an accepted COVID-19 vaccine to board a flight 
to the United States.

CONFIRMATION 
Physical confirmation of registration is mandatory. The Team Manager is responsible to 
communicate the presence of the National Team. In case of late arrival please contact 
OF ATTENDANCE  the IFSC Event Delegate as soon as possible.  
 
Even though the confirmation of attendance for Boulder and Lead takes place on 
separate days, the event accreditation for registered team members can be picked up on 
any day when the accreditation is open. With your accreditation badge, you have access 
to the spectator area of the event during the whole week. Accreditation opening hours 
will be published on our website. 
NOTE: If you pick up your accreditation for the event already before the designated 
registration time, your attendance is not confirmed automatically! You will still need to 
confirm your attendance, either in person during the above-mentioned registration time 
or via message to the Event Delegate.

SUPPORT TEAM

GUIDANCE

Officials as soon as is practicably possible

may not guide other athletes in finals.
